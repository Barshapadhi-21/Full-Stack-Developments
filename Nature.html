Nature....

Nature refers to everything that exists in the world without being made by humans. It includes plants, animals, mountains, rivers, oceans, forests, air, and the changing seasons. Nature provides us with fresh air to breathe, water to drink, and food to survive. It is also beautiful and peaceful, and people often visit natural places to relax and feel calm. Protecting nature is important, because many natural resources are limited and animals and plants depend on a healthy environment to live.
